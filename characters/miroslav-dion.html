Character Info

Miroslav Dion

Older than most nations and wealthier than several, Miroslav Dion built the Ironclad Consortium from a soldier's discipline and a predator's patience. He has outlived kings, financed empires, and buried every rival foolish enough to test him. Now an old debt and an older enemy draw him toward Newport Maeve, where the city's gleaming progress hides a hunger that feeds on civilization itself.
