Character Info

Miroslav Dion

He was a soldier lifetimes ago, then he became a Blood Blessed Predator. He learned to control the hunger and built a unique society of special people. Now he is a Legal Mogul, pulling the strings of Underground syndicates. With his vast influence and wealth, he is the last contingency of the all-knowing One True against incoming dark threats.
