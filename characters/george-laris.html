Character Info

George L'Aris

George L'Aris carries out the Consortium's will in the places polite society does not look. A vampire bound to Dion's strict order, he knows the cost of the power he wields and the leash that comes with it. Loyalty keeps him sharp; thirst keeps him honest about what he is. In a city of masks, he is one of the few who admits to the monster underneath.
