Character Info

Aland

Newport Maeve is built on hunger, and Aland learned young how to feed it just enough to slip a hand into its pocket. A street con artist with quick wits and quicker feet, he survives on charm, nerve, and an instinct for which lies people most want to believe. The city has chewed up sharper operators than him — but few as hard to hold on to.
