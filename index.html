The World of Newport Maeve

A steampunk fantasy realm where ancient magic meets industrial progress

The Confederacy

A vast political union spanning multiple nations, bound by law and bureaucracy. The Confederacy represents order, progress, and the careful balance of power maintained through hidden networks and strategic influence.

Newport Maeve

The crown jewel of the Confederacy—a city of brass and invention where ambition meets steel. Built at the confluence of the Zarina River and the coast, Newport Maeve stands as a beacon of progress and a testbed for the future.

The One True

An ancient faith born from divine intervention during the demon wars. The Church of the One True wields significant power, yet mysteries remain about the true nature of the divine entities that walk among mortals.

Blood Blessing

A supernatural gift—or curse—that grants immortality and superhuman abilities. The BloodBlessed are rare, powerful, and bound by ancient laws. They exist in the shadows, shaping history from behind the scenes.

The Underground

A hidden network of fixers, brokers, and operatives who exist beyond the law. Where official channels fail, the Underground succeeds—swiftly, precisely, and with absolute discretion.

Ironclad Consortium

A global network of influence spanning industry, information, and power. The Consortium operates in plain sight, a legitimate business empire that conceals far deeper ambitions and darker secrets.

Miroslav Dion

The Architect of Centuries

[Placeholder: miroslav-dion.jpg]

Once Human, Now BloodBlessed

Miroslav Dion is an immortal vampire who has walked the earth for centuries. Born as a foot soldier in a forgotten war, he was transformed by divine intervention into something far greater—and far more dangerous.

Title: Owner of the Ironclad Consortium, Master of the Underground
Age: Appears in his 40s; actually centuries old
Status: Unknown to the public; feared by those who know
Abilities: Enhanced senses, blood control, regeneration, dominion presence

Dion survived where others perished. He adapted when the world shifted. He lost everything, rebuilt, and lost it all again. What drives him is not hope or ambition—it is the stubborn instinct not to stop.

He has shaped the Confederacy from the shadows, maintaining balance through law and hidden networks. He is a strategist, a manipulator, and a man bound by an ancient debt to forces beyond mortal comprehension.
